Privacy Policy

Intro Deck is a Telegram-native private people directory and intro request product. This Privacy Policy explains what information Intro Deck processes, why it is processed, and how it is used when you interact with the Intro Deck bot, website, and LinkedIn sign-in flow.

1. Information we process

  • Telegram identifiers and account metadata needed to operate the bot.
  • LinkedIn sign-in and identity bootstrap data received through the LinkedIn authorization flow.
  • Profile information that you add or edit inside the bot, such as display name, headline, company, city, about, skills, and listing status.
  • Intro request records, inbox decisions, and related contact-unlock state needed to run the workflow.
  • Operational data such as notification receipts, retry metadata, anti-abuse guards, and security logs.

2. Why we process this information

  • To authenticate users and bootstrap identity through LinkedIn sign-in.
  • To create and maintain user profiles and directory listings inside Telegram.
  • To deliver intro requests, inbox actions, decisions, and contact-unlock flows.
  • To keep the service secure, prevent abuse, and maintain operational reliability.
  • To diagnose failures and support retry, audit, and service integrity workflows.

3. How profile visibility works

Profiles are shown in the directory only when they are marked as both active and listed. Contact details are not opened by default. The service follows a privacy-first contract where contact access is unlocked only through the product's decision-aware intro workflow.

4. Data sharing

Intro Deck does not sell personal information. Data is shared only as needed to operate the workflow, such as with infrastructure providers that host the service, LinkedIn for sign-in, and Telegram for bot delivery. Profile and intro information may be shown to other users only within the product features that you use, such as listed directory cards, sent intro requests, received intro requests, and post-decision contact surfaces.

5. Retention and security

Intro Deck keeps operational and workflow data only for as long as reasonably needed to run the service, maintain history, support anti-abuse protections, and investigate failures. Security controls include webhook secret validation, short-lived anti-abuse guards, protected operational routes, and durable notification receipt tracking.

6. Your choices

  • You may edit your profile fields inside the bot.
  • You may control whether your profile is listed and active in the directory.
  • You may stop using the bot at any time.

7. Contact

For privacy questions or requests related to Intro Deck, contact the operator through the official Intro Deck LinkedIn page or the official Telegram bot at @introdeckbot.
