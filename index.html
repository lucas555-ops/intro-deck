FAQ

What people usually want to know.

What is Intro Deck?

Intro Deck is a private professional directory in Telegram designed for warm introductions and controlled contact flow.

Is this public?

No. The product is designed around private discovery and deliberate access rather than public contact exposure.

Why use LinkedIn?

LinkedIn acts as the identity bootstrap so the system starts from a stronger trust layer, while public fields remain Telegram-managed.

How does contact work?

Contact is handled through structured paths such as intro requests, gated direct access, or private DM relay depending on the profile contract.

What happens after I open the bot?

You connect identity, complete your card, discover people, and move into the appropriate contact path inside Telegram.
